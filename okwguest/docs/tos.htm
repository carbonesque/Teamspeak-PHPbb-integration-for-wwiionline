OKA TeamSpeak Server Terms of Service

IMPORTANT: By using this server, you agree to observe these rules. Failure to complay may be cause for removal.


1. Use of the OKA Teamspeak server is a privilege, not a right. Access to the server, certain channels, and special admin rights are not guaranteed. You are expected to act mature and respect other players.

2. Use of the OKA Teamspeak server is restricted to matters relating to the WWIIOL Axis community only. You may not use this server for other games. You may not use this server while playing WWIIOL as Allied.

3. Your Teamspeak account ‘Login Name’ must be exactly the same as your in-game name in WWIIOL. Your “Nickname’ as entered in your Teamspeak client must contain your in-game name as well, but must be preceded by your squad name or initials. (i.e. “6PD playername”)

4. You may have ONLY one account on the OKA Teamspeak server.

5. You must logout of Teamspeak when you are not using it. Idlers (over 2 hrs) will be kicked.

6. Other rules may be applied by the OKW chain of command to particular channels. These additional rules are displayed in the information (right-hand) pane of Teamspeak. When using these channels, you are expected to observe these rules. Some channels may disallow cursing or other adult language. Some channels may have strict comms protocol.

7. OKW Officers & game managers are governed under an additional set of rules beyond those stated here. Officers are to consult their Unit Commanders (or visit the Officer's Club) for more details.

The OKA Teamspeak server is not a service offered by WWIIOL or PLAYNET, but a private server hosted –and paid for-- by Grizwald for the benefit of the community.
